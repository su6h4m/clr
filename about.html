About ClearlyU

Our story, mission, and values

From the desk of CEO

I’m Rishabh, the founder of clearlyU. This brand was born not in a lab or boardroom, but from my own struggle with skincare. Growing up with oily yet sensitive skin, I battled tanning, pigmentation, and dryness—especially during Indian summers. Despite trying well-known Indian and international brands, nothing truly worked. That’s when I realized: these products weren’t made for us. They weren’t designed for the extremes of our climate or the unique needs of Indian skin.

clearlyU began with a simple belief: skincare should be clean, effective, and accessible—not a luxury reserved for a few. I saw how most brands focused more on marketing than real results, using harsh ingredients and pricing out everyday consumers. So, I set out to build a brand that puts people before profit, focusing on performance-driven formulas with science-backed, gentle ingredients tailored for Indian conditions.

At clearlyU, our mission is to deliver visible results without compromises. We offer premium-quality, chemical-free skincare that understands your skin and speaks your language. Because when it comes to skincare, we believe purity, transparency, and affordability should never be optional. With clearlyU, you’re not just choosing a product—you’re choosing confidence, clarity, and care.
